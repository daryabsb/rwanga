## Script Overview

**Total Scenes:** 43  
**Total Shots:** 340

### Logline

Nali, a playwright and theater director who has lost faith in love and human connection, relies on AI for companionship. He meets a girl online and invites her to his remote garden house. When she dies unexpectedly overnight, Nali panics and makes increasingly desperate choices to hide her body — only to discover her death was from natural causes all along.

## Current Production Phase

### Test Shoot: Scene 12

**Scene 12 Summary:**

Gesha enters Nali's strangely decorated garden house for the first time. She discovers taxidermy, masks, and cryptic notes. A locked door raises questions. The scene establishes the house as a character and seeds Gesha's growing unease about who Nali really is.

**Proposed Shot Breakdown:** 15 shots  
**Status:** Pending director approval

**Production Rationale:**

Producers requested the director test one scene with real actors on location to validate the visual language, pacing, and actor chemistry before full production commences.
