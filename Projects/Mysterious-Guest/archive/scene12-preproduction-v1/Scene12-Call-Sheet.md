## Important Notes

1. **PHONES ON SILENT.** All crew and cast — no exceptions. A ringing phone during the locked door take (12.14) will cost 10 minutes of reset and emotional rebuild.

2. **NO SMOKING inside the location.** Smoke lingers and affects continuity (and actors' breathing in close-ups). Smoking area outside, away from entrance.

3. **QUIET ON SET** discipline is critical for this scene. 7 of 15 shots have dialogue, and 3 shots (12.3, 12.5, 12.15) depend on audible SILENCE. The boom is always hot. Assume you're being recorded at all times.

4. **CONTINUITY PHOTOS** will be taken by the script supervisor before Block 1 and after any wardrobe/hair adjustment. Both actors: front, both profiles, full body.

5. **FOOTAGE BACKUP** happens during the final 20 minutes of wrap. All camera cards and audio files copied to TWO separate drives before anyone leaves. No exceptions. No "I'll do it at home."

6. **LOCATION RESPECT.** This is someone's property. Leave it as we found it (or better). All wall mounts removed, holes filled if possible. All crew trash collected. Equipment scratches on floors = our problem.
