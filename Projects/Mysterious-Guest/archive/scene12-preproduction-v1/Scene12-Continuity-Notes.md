### Lighting Continuity

The scene is evening interior with warm practicals. Because blocks are shot over 4+ hours, the actual daylight outside may change. This should NOT affect the interior look if blackout material is properly deployed on all windows. However:

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Sunlight shifts visible through curtain gaps | LED Panel 2 (faked window light) must remain consistent across all blocks. Mark its position, angle, and intensity. |
| Practical lamp positions bumped by crew | Photograph pendant lamp positions from below (ceiling reference) at the start of the day. Check between blocks. |
| Dimmer settings shifted | Label each dimmer with tape and a number. Write the number on the call sheet. |
