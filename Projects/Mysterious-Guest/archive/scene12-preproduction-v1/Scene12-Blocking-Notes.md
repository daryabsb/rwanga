# Blocking & Floor Plan Notes — Scene 12

**Film:** Mysterious Guest
**Scene:** 12 — INT. Garden House — Evening
**Document:** Accompanies overhead floor plan diagram

---

## Room Layout (Approximate 4m × 8m)

**NORTH WALL (Back):** Heavy cream curtains covering a window. Mounted deer head (TO ADD) center above curtains. Two doors on the right side: a LOCKED DOOR (dark blue/black, northeast corner) and NALI'S ROOM (brown door, beside it). A large painted canvas leans against the wall between the doors area and the curtains. A painting of a tree hangs near the locked door.

**SOUTH WALL (Entrance):** Main entrance door, center. This is where Nali and Gesha enter the scene.

**EAST WALL (Right when entering):** Tall wooden shelving unit filled with glassware, pottery, bottles, ceramics — this is the kitchen/counter area. A metal display shelf with dishes and plates stands beside it, slightly forward into the room. Pendant lights hang from the ceiling above this area.

**WEST WALL (Left when entering):** Sofa with colorful cushions and patchwork fabrics (upper section). Stacked books/bookcase (lower section). TV mounted or placed on a stand. Photo frames and artworks hung on the wall. Theater masks (TO ADD) hung near the bookcase in shadow. Daggers and curved sword (TO ADD) mounted between photos.

**CENTER:** Wooden dining table with 6-8 white/cream chairs on red Persian rugs. A large pendant lamp hangs directly above. The table is the geographic anchor of the room — most shots reference it.

**BETWEEN TABLE AND BACK WALL:** Desk/work area — a table with an open laptop, scattered papers (including the "Stories are waiting" note), books, bottles, and various objects. This is Nali's creative workspace.

## Gesha's Blocking Path (6 Stops)

| Stop | Location | Shots | Action |
|------|----------|-------|--------|
| 1 | Entrance doorway | 12.1 | Enters behind Nali, stops at threshold, begins scanning room |
| 2 | Dining table area | 12.3, 12.6 | Puts bags on table, reacts to the room, first dialogue with Nali |
| 3 | Near bookcase / masks (West wall) | 12.7, 12.9 | Discovers masks, questions Nali about them |
| 4 | Desk / work area | 12.11 | Finds "Stories are waiting" paper, questions Nali |
| 5 | Back to deer head (North wall) | 12.13 | Returns to deer head with calmer curiosity, asks about the sculptures |
| 6 | Door area (Northeast) | 12.14 | Tries locked door, gets redirected to Nali's room, enters and closes door |

**Movement character:** Gesha's path is a loop — she enters south, drifts left (west) toward the masks, moves forward (north) to the desk, circles back to the deer head, then moves right to the doors. It's an exploration pattern, like an animal circling a new territory before committing. Her pace should slow as she gets deeper into the space.

## Nali's Blocking

Nali's movement is minimal — he is the still center while Gesha orbits.

| Position | Shots | Action |
|----------|-------|--------|
| Kitchen/shelf area (East wall) | 12.1, 12.4 | Enters first, moves to kitchen to set down bags, watches Gesha |
| Same area, leaning on counter | 12.6, 12.10, 12.12 | Dialogue — stays put, relaxed posture, gestures toward rooms |
| Center of living room | 12.15 | Alone after Gesha goes to change, picks up phone and meat, exits toward front door |

**Movement character:** Nali barely moves. He enters, parks himself at the kitchen counter, and stays. This is deliberate — he is the host, the spider at the center. His stillness contrasts with Gesha's restless exploration.
