**Film:** Mysterious Guest
**Scene:** 12 — INT. Garden House — Evening
**Style Direction:** Pencil sketch / cinematic storyboard style, high contrast, dramatic shadows, film noir influenced. Widescreen 2.39:1 aspect ratio for all frames.

## Character Reference Notes

**NALI:** Man, late 30s, tall and lean build, dark brown hair swept back, full trimmed beard, brown eyes, brooding/intense expression. Wearing a casual linen button-up shirt, sleeves slightly rolled. Carries himself with quiet confidence, artistic demeanor.

**GESHA:** Young woman, early-to-mid 20s, long curly honey-blonde hair falling past shoulders, green-hazel eyes, fair skin, expressive face. Wearing a casual outfit — dark top, carrying bags and a small suitcase. Sunglasses pushed up on her head. Naturally warm face but capable of showing unease.

## Location Reference Notes

**THE ROOM:** A long rectangular space, low dark ceiling with exposed beams and pendant industrial lights. LEFT WALL: wooden shelving unit filled with pottery, ceramics, bottles, small sculptures; a tall metal display shelf with glass objects and candles. CENTER: a wooden dining table with white modern chairs on a large red Persian rug. BACK WALL: heavy curtains (cream/yellow) covering a window, a dark pendant lamp. RIGHT WALL: photos pinned/hung in clusters, books stacked, a sofa/couch area with colorful cushions and fabric. FAR RIGHT: a large painted canvas leaning against the wall, a wardrobe/closet with doors. FLOOR: layered red and blue Persian rugs on white tile. The space feels cluttered, artistic, bohemian — like a theater artist's living museum.

**PROPS TO ADD (not in current room — must be imagined into the space):** A mounted taxidermy deer head on the back wall above the curtains. Pale expressionless theater masks (3-4) hung near the bookshelf area in shadow. Daggers and a curved sword mounted on the right wall between the photos. Small alien-like/abstract sculptures on shelves. A desk with an open laptop and scattered papers in the right-side area near the sofa.
