## Scene 12 — INT. GARDEN HOUSE — EVENING

**Film:** Mysterious Guest (میوانێکی نادیار)
**Director:** Sarwar Muhedin
**Status:** LOCKED — Approved, April 2026
**Version:** v1.0

---

### Scene Description

Gesha enters Nali's strangely decorated garden house for the first time. She discovers taxidermy heads, theatrical masks, and cryptic notes. A locked door raises questions she doesn't voice. The scene establishes the house as a third character and seeds Gesha's growing unease about who Nali really is.

### Emotional Structure

**Movement 1 — THE ENTRANCE:** Wonder turning to unease. The camera reveals the space through Gesha's experience.

**Movement 2 — THE DISCOVERY:** Curiosity and fear. Each discovery peels back a layer of Nali's psyche. A quiet power dynamic.

**Movement 3 — THE LOCKED DOOR:** Tension and deflection. The scene ends with separation — two people retreating to their private selves.
